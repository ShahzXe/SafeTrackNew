About SafeTrack

SafeTrack is a trusted contracting company in Saudi Arabia, delivering high-quality Electrical, Civil, and Mechanical solutions. Established in 2008, SafeTrack has built a strong reputation for reliability and excellence in the construction industry, successfully executing projects across various sectors.


Headquartered in Saudi Arabia, SafeTrack takes pride in managing a diverse portfolio of projects with a strong commitment to quality, safety, and efficiency. Our skilled team of professionals ensures that every project is completed on time while maintaining the highest industry standards.


In addition to our contracting services, SafeTrack is dedicated to providing innovative technical solutions and continuous customer support. We invest in cutting-edge technology and experienced personnel to meet the evolving needs of our clients.


At SafeTrack, we prioritize long-term relationships with our clients, employees, and suppliers. Our mission is to contribute to Saudi Arabia’s infrastructure growth and uphold our commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

    Strategic Partnership: Safe Track and K&B’s Electric Unite

      Safe Track is proud to announce a strategic joint venture with K&B’s Electric a company that has been a leader in the Pakistan market since its establishment in 2006. K&B’s Electric is renowned for its exceptional services across Civil Construction , Electrical Installation & Maintenance and IT Solutions, and has built a solid reputation for delivering high-quality projects on time, with unmatched precision and professionalism. This collaboration combines Safe Track’s expertise in safety and security solutions with K&B’s Electric extensive experience and trusted presence in the Pakistani market. Together, we aim to deliver innovative, reliable, and comprehensive engineering solutions to clients in both Pakistan and Saudi Arabia.

    Building Success through Strong Organizational Structure

      The organizational structure of SAFE TRACK COMPANY LIMITED is meticulously designed to ensure seamless management and operational efficiency. At the helm are the esteemed Board Members, followed by the General Manager who oversees distinct departments such as Business Development, Accounting & Finance, Project Operations, Administration, and Procurement. Each department is led by dedicated managers, fostering a collaborative environment that enhances productivity and innovation. This structured approach underscores SAFE TRACK's commitment to delivering high-quality projects on time, adhering to stringent safety standards, and ensuring unparalleled customer satisfaction.
